Recipe #1: Ceviche

  • Time:10 minutes
  • Serves:2
Ingredients Quantity
fresh fish 1 kg
onion 0.25 kg
lemon juice 10 ml
chilli 2 units
salt up to you

Step 1: Wash and dry the fish. Cut the fish into cubes of approximately 2 cm. Remove any remaining skin, scales or spines. It is important that you only have cubes of lean meat similar in size.

Step 2: Wash the lemons and limes. Squeeze them into a large bowl. Strain the fresh lime juice and lemon juice to remove any seeds.

Step 3: This lime juice will serve to cook the fish by the marination process. Put the fish cubes into the juice marinade.Add onion, chili and coriander, ginger, a pinch of salt and stir. Cover the ceviche again and leave it in the fridge for about 10 min and eat it :D

Source: EATPERU:Ceviche

Recipe #2: Lomo saltado

  • Time:40 minutes
  • Serves:4
Ingredients Quantity
sirloin steak cubes 8 ounces
red onion chopped 0.5 kg
tomatoes chopped 2 units
fresh garlic paste 1 tsp
white rice cooked up to you literally
thick potato slices 11 ounces

Step 1: Heat a Pan. Fry the potato slices in the vegetable oil and set aside until needed. Cut the sirloin steak into strips or cubes. Marinate them in the cumin, salt, the 4 tablespoons of vinegar, atablespoon of olive oil, black pepper and the soy sauce for 10 minutes beforecooking.

Step 2: Bring a tablespoon of olive oil toa very high heat in the wok. Strain and remove the steak strips from the marinade, and fry them over a high heat for around 4-5 minutes or untilsealed.Add the onion, ají amarillo chilli pepper and the garlic paste to the wok. Fry for 1 minute whilst continuously moving the wok, and add the beef stock.

Step 3: Add the tomato and fry the ingredients in the wok for 30 seconds. Finally add the ground oregano and French fries, tossing everything together for another 10 seconds in order to integrate all the ingredients.Serve immediately with white rice and decorate with a few cilantro leaves. Sit back and enjoy the flavor of timeless classic of Peruvian cuisine!

Source: EATPERU :LOMOSALTADO

Recipe #3: Aji de gallina

  • Time:60 minutes
  • Serves:2
Ingredients Quantity
chili 4 un
onion 0.25 kg
chicken 1/5 kg
egg 2 units
lettuce up to you

Step 1: Cut the ají amarillo chili peppers in half and remove the veins (ribs) and seeds. Holding them with a tong, grill the peppers directly on a high heat flame until the skin is cooked and black in parts. Rinse the peppers and remove the skin, then chop roughly.In a frying pan, add some vegetable oil and lightly fry the ají peppers, chopped white or red onion, garlic and pecans. Transfer the mixture to a blender and blend.

Step 2: Pour the contents of the blender into a frying pan and add the shredded breast. Stir well and cook until it achieves the right consistency. Add the grated parmesan cheese and the evaporated milk just before turning off the heat.Boil the potatoes, unpeeled and with 3 tablespoons of salt and leave to cool.

Step 3: Boil the eggs until hard and leave to cool.Place a lettuce leaf on the plate, followed by 4 generous slices of potato per serving and a portion of rice. Serve over the white rice and potatoes. Serve with one or two slices of boiled egg and black olives.

Source: EATPERU:AJIGALLINA
